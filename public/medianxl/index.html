Don't Starve Together!

"Don't Starve Together" is a whimsical multiplayer survival game where you and your buddies are plopped into a bizarre world filled with quirky critters, unpredictable weather, and a ravenous appetite for teamwork, all set to serve up a side dish of laughs as you try to stay well-fed and alive.

Pick up simple resources, craft basic tools, weapons, traps and whatnot!

Create your kooky haven by building a base to escape the wacky, ever-threatening world!

Slaughter spiders, rabbits, hounds and many different monsters to stay sane in a crazy, cartoonish world!
